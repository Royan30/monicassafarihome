About Us

Monica's Safari Home Co. was founded with a mission to bring stylish and affordable furniture to homes across Kenya. We pride ourselves in offering unique, handpicked furniture pieces that reflect African beauty, comfort, and utility.

Based in Utawala, Nairobi, we operate as a local dropshipping company, ensuring fast delivery and smooth Mpesa payment options. We are committed to delivering both quality and value to every home.
